Software development

Along my research and observatory duty activities I have a great interest in the development of softwares using the Python language. Most of my research works come with software/module releases. I am a great supporter of open-source and open-science and try to release as much as possible the tools I am using to do my research.
You can find here the list of the tools I have developped and the link to their respective documentation.
